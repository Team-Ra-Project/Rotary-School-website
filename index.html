Welcome to Rotary School

Learning that goes beyond the classroom.

Education is not just about achieving good grades — it’s about inspiring curiosity, building confidence, and preparing children for life. At Rotary School Uran, every lesson, activity, and experience is designed to help students discover their strengths, think independently, and develop the skills needed to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Our dedicated teachers, engaging curriculum, and supportive learning environment empower every child to become academically strong, socially responsible, and emotionally confident. We don’t just prepare students for examinations — we prepare them for life.
