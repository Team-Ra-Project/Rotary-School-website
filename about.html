A legacy of learning since 1990.

Our story

Founded on a simple belief: every child deserves quality education.

People of Uran once faced real difficulty educating their children in an English medium school, due to high donations and fees. In response, Rotary Education Society was formed to open an English medium school for the students of this area — so they could face the challenges of the years ahead with confidence.

As education is a human right, we admit children of every category without donation. We never ask whether a parent is literate or illiterate, or whether their financial position is strong or weak. We give admission to all, and take responsibility for their all-round development through trained, motivated teachers and advancing technology.

Our mission

💡

Our journey

Milestones along the way

16 August 1990

Rotary Education Society establishes Rotary English Medium High School in Bori, Uran, with a mission to make English medium education accessible to all.

Expansion to Junior College

The school grows to offer Junior College programs in Science and Commerce, extending the journey through Std 12.

Modern labs & technology

Science, Maths, and Computer labs are introduced, along with campus-wide CCTV surveillance and e-learning tools.

1,300+ students today

Today, Rotary School Uran is home to over 1,300 students guided by 42+ dedicated teachers across KG to Junior College.

Looking ahead: Senior College

We are working to build a proposed Senior College, requiring further investment in infrastructure — a goal made possible through community support.
