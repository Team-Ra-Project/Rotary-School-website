Why we ask

A self-financed institution with a big responsibility.

Rotary Education Society was established with a vision to provide quality English medium education to every child, regardless of financial background. As a self-financed institution, we do not receive government grants — yet remain committed to making education accessible and affordable. We aspire to build a Senior College, requiring an estimated ₹3 crores for infrastructure.

  • ✓ Expand educational opportunities
  • ✓ Develop modern infrastructure
  • ✓ Support deserving students
  • ✓ Build the proposed Senior College
  • ✓ Continue empowering future generations
