Academics, answered

Rotary School Uran is a government-recognised institution following the Maharashtra State Board curriculum from KG through Std 12.

We offer both Science and Commerce streams at the Junior College level, for Std 11 and 12.

Small-group attention, dedicated subject teachers, and regular progress tracking help every student get the support they need.
